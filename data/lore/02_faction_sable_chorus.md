# Faction Canon: The Sable Chorus

Source ID: sable_chorus

The Sable Chorus is a clandestine order of singers, smugglers, and oath-breakers active in the marsh roads around Harrowmere. Its members use black reed-flutes to send coded phrases across water at night.

The Sable Chorus wanted access to the Sunken Temple's lower archive because the archive held debt ledgers, land grants, and erased verdicts from the old salt courts. These documents could ruin several surviving noble houses.

No canon source proves that the Sable Chorus caused the Bell Flood. Surviving temple notes only show that two Chorus-linked smugglers were seen near the east sluice two days before the flood. A broken reed-flute was later found in the collapsed sluice chamber.

Maelor distrusts the Sable Chorus but refuses to accuse them as fact without proof. He calls them "probable hands, not proven hands."
